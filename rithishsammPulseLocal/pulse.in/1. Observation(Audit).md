This document is to observe and recollect what are all the existing workloads running, the operational costs, resources consumption, environments, nature of the workloads, workloads categorization, identify stale resources - focuses on establishing **complete visibility** into the organization’s infrastructure across AWS and on-prem environments.

With all that, will make decisions on
- Identify **all running workloads**
- Understand **resource utilization patterns**
- Analyze **cost distribution**
- Classify workloads based on **function and behavior**
- Detect **inefficiencies, unused, and stale resources**

This serves as the **baseline for optimization and modernization decisions**.
